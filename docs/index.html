Otoroshi Biscuit Studio

A set of Otoroshi plugins to integrate Biscuit Tokens

Download latest version

Biscuit Verifiers

Biscuit Verifiers allow you to validate tokens securely and flexibly. They ensure that permissions and policies are respected at all times.

Attenuators

Attenuators enable you to refine or reduce the capabilities of tokens, granting only the minimal required permissions for secure operations.

Remote Facts Loader

The Remote Facts Loader allows integration of external data seamlessly into token verifications, expanding the scope and dynamism of your system.

Hierarchical Delegation

Biscuit tokens support hierarchical delegation, enabling flexible delegation of permissions while maintaining strict control over policies.

Compact Design

Biscuit tokens are compact and designed for efficiency, making them ideal for resource-constrained environments and fast transmissions.

Auditable Policies

Policies embedded in Biscuit tokens are easily auditable, ensuring that security and compliance requirements are met with transparency.
